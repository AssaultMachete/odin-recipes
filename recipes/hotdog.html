Hotdog Rolls Recipe

How to make hotdog rolls at home guide

Ingrediants

  • 8 slices of Bread
  • 2 Tablespoons of Mustard
  • 4 thin slices of Cheese
  • 8 beef hotdogs

Step

  1. Gather Ingrediants
  2. Make the rolls
  3. Fill it with the Seasonings
  4. Roll the bread with hotdogs
  5. Bake it
