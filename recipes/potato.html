Potato Soup Recipe

How to make Potato soup with only 4-ingrediant

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 pound bacon
  • 1 bag of potatoes
  • 30 ounces chicken broth
  • your fav Seasonings

Steps

  1. Place the bacon in a bowl
  2. pour the potatoes & chicken into the bowl
  3. put the Soup & Seasonings
  4. Heat the mixture
